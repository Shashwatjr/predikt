# Data Retention Policy

- Raw GPS events: 24-72 hours
- Aggregated progress: 12-24 months
- Prediction records: 24 months
- Aura and Clout transactions: 36 months
- Campaign analytics: 36 months
- Audit logs: 6 years
- Consent records: life of account plus legal retention period
- Deleted user personal data: anonymize within 30 days

This is a policy baseline for MVP+ and should be operationalized with retention jobs in a later phase.
